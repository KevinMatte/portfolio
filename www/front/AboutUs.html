Kevin Matte Software Designs is a family run business that incorporated in 2003.

Currently, the main Systems Business Analyst is Kevin Matte (myself). I have a BSc from the University of British Columbia with a major in Mathematical Computing.

I started my career in software development back in 1987 as a software contractor working on a school scheduling system for students. I moved on to a telephone operator scheduling system and then an aircraft heavy maintenance scheduling system.

Later, I started a career in telecommunication software at Microtel Pacific Research (Burnaby, Canada), which was bought by Applied Digital Access (San Diego: where I was happily transferred), which was bought by TTC, which became Acterna, which was bought by JDS Uniphase.

I wore many hats, project manager, project prime, ISO 9000 auditor, team leader, mentor senior software developer, and several titles as company changes and reorganizations occurred and worked with many languages and UNIX/Microsoft platforms (but not Windows .NET).

I work well in a team or alone (very alone, remotely and the only one left on a project, I might add). I really enjoy my work, the people and all the challenges and rewards.
